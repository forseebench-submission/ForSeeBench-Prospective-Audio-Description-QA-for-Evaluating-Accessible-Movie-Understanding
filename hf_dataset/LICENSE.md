# Anonymous Review Access Terms

ForSeeBench derived benchmark files, documentation, and included figures are provided for anonymous paper review, reproducibility checking, and non-commercial research evaluation.

You may use the released derived benchmark artifacts to inspect the submission, run validation and scoring scripts, reproduce reported benchmark evaluations where model and source-data access are available, and compare research systems under the documented evaluation protocol.

You may not redistribute raw source assets, attempt to reconstruct restricted movie media, use the artifact for deployment or accessibility-certification claims, or represent benchmark accuracy as a substitute for blind and low-vision user studies.

Original MAD/MAD-eval and underlying movie source assets remain governed by their original providers' terms. This release does not redistribute those raw source assets.
